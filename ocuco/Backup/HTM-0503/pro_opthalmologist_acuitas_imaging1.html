Acuitas Imaging
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Acuitas Imaging is a module available within Acuitas patient management system. It handles everything to do with Capture, Review & Processing of ophthalmic fundus, anterior & other related images.
  • Digital anterior and Fundus imaging:
  • Image capture from most of the popular Fundus & Anterior cameras available on the market.
  • Image view & manipulations (Zoom/Pan/ Rotate/Flip/Crop, Contrast/Brightness enhancement, Colour enhancement, Colour filters)
  • Fundus image montage (Fundus map) & anaglyph stereo
  • Image annotation & measurements (with Cup to Disc ratio)
  • Single or multiple image reporting with notes & annotations included
