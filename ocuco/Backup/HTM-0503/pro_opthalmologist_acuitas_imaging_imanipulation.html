All of the image manipulation operations are performed on a duplicate image,keeping the original intact.
Apply the following filter effects
  • Bleed – Reduce the red saturation, without affecting the overall colour
  • Bump map – Get a reverse gray scale rendering of the image,highlighting the blood vessels with an embossed
    effect
  • Blur – Smooth over sharp image for printing purpose
  • Edge detect – Automatically mark & highlight edges
  • Greyscale – Convert a colour image to gray scale
  • Negative – Invert the colour to bring out hidden details
  • Red – Isolate the red channel (useful for anaglyphic stereo pairing)
  • Red free – Fully remove the red channel
  • Sharpen – Reverse of Blur filter
Enhance the Brightness, Contrast with quick access options
Adjust Colour & Gamma using RGB, HSV & HSL colour models
Apply the above manipulations to selected regions of the images with the following selection tools: Rectangular, Elliptical,
Freehand & Magic wand (auto selects all pixels with similar colour).
Crop a selected region of the image.
View tonal range of the image with multi-channel histogram.
Most of the manipulations performed on the image get logged in to notes, attached with individual images, for easy
reproduction of a particular effect on other images & additional information can also be manually recorded in the notes
section.
Print Fundus images, in portrait or landscape format, with the black frame around the image automatically removed, to
save printer ink. Also print selected regions or zoom in area, directly.
