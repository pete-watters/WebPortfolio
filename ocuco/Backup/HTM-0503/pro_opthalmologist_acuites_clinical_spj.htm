Acuitas Clinical follows the complete patient journey. All roles within the practice use the application, which allows for
continuity at all parts of the patient’s journey.
The Diary
The Diary is the hub of any practice. The day develops through the diary and is an initial indicator of practice past and
future performance.
A good Diary has:
  • Access from all computers
  • Flexibility
  • Configurability
  • Colour coding
Key Features
  • Links with the patient database ensuring that the correct patient is booked for the appointment
  • Configurable for each resource for each day
  • Multi-Diary view to see one Diary for six days or all diaries for one day
  • ‘ Find Nest Appointment ’ feature with time, OO and date parameters
  • Colour coding for appointment type and status of appointment
  • ‘ Block Outs ’ for vacations, public holidays or visits by non patients
  • ‘ Post It ’ notes for appointment and patient ‘ to dos ’
  • Integrates with query to allow analysis on appointments
  • SMS Texting features for configurable advance reminders
  • Create appointment for patient in one click
  • Amend appointment lengths as required
Key Benefits
  • At a glance visibility of the appointment, why they are visiting and the stage of journey
  • Book appointments at time that suit the patient (or you)
  • Smooth workflow in the practice
  • Optometrists finds patients easily and no re-keying
  • Increase revenues by actively managing DNA’s
  • Saves administration time and Tippex!
  • Patient’s next appointment is immediately visible on patients record, no more leafing through paper diaries
  • Reduce DNA’s with automatic distribution of reminder SMS
Finding a Patient Record or Creating a New Patient
Finding your patients while on the phone or serving other customers is part of your daily routine
It is important for patient search and creation of patients to be:
  • Fast
  • Reliable
  • Accurate – no duplications
The Patient Search
Creating a new patient either through the Diary while making an appointment or through the main application forces the user
through the search screen in order to ensure that the patient is not already registered.
Key Features
  • Search for a patient using one of or a mixture of the following parameters; Full name, Postcode, Address (and parts of),
    DOB, PID, Last Visit, Telephone no, DD Reference, Alias and Insurance references.
  • Incremental search narrows list as you type, you don’t have to type the full name to find a patient
Key Benefits
  • Quick access to your patients records
  • Never lose a patient, even if you don’t remember the name
Through the Diary
Key Features
  • Search function built into Diary
  • Creation of patients through appointment time slot or search function
  • Postcode lookup integration
Key Benefits
  • No need to create a full record for the patient for making an appointment
  • Allows the creation of an appointment before asking the patient all their details, patient just wants to know the date
    and time first.
  • No need to leave Diary to create a record
Key Features
  • Contained list of basic details for the registration of patient
  • Self learning lists for quick data entry
  • Pre-configured default town and county
  • Colour code for required fields
Key Benefits
  • Faster data entry
  • Ensuring the information is captured allows the business to follow through on communications
  • Contained list enables user to capture all data required.
