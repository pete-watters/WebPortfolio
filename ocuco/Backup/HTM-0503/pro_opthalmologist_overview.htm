Ophthalmology is a complex business: attracting new patients and recalling existing ones, performing eye examinations,
and after having satisfied each patient, providing targeted aftercare so that the hard-earned patient remains loyal to your
practice.

Ocuco have gathered and applied knowledge and expertise in software for healthcare practices for over a decade. The
experience gained in other sectors is applied to the whole range of software products, and benefits our products for
Ophthalmologists.
The main trademarks of Ocuco products for ophthalmologists’ practices and hospitals are
  • Suitability for purpose
  • Extremely high availability and mission-critical reliability
  • Patient Records Security
Among the practical and functional elements of Acuitas that can assist in achieving better results are the following:
  • Comprehensive Examinations with graphics keeping patient care a high priority
  • Thorough Patient Recall – using all modes of communication, letter, SMS, email and phone
  • Effective target communications with your patients
  • ‘ Business Intelligence ’ providing the relevant information at your finger tips
  • Links with Fundus and anterior cameras allowing to share expensive equipment
  • Links with Focimeters, phoroptors, autorefractors and tonometers for quick data entry
  • Paperless saves space, time and bottom line costs
Acuitas meets the challenge of comprehensively computerising ophthalmology practices with a choice of software packages:

Acuitas Clinical
Patient Relationship Management including a fully paperless patient journey

This is recommended for the independent ophthalmologist who:

Want - their practice to run more efficiently and paperlessly, desire to be at the leading edge, want to enable staff to provide
a better level of patient service, welcome a less administrative heavy channel of communication with patients, wish to share
expensive diagnostic equipment, reassurance that that patient data is secure, can see the value in freeing staff and utilising
each member to share and enhance the patients journey and strive to have more control and visibility of staff/practice
performance.

Do not want – tight control on stock, locked down cash management, links with other practices.

Acuitas Imaging
Anterior and Fundus imaging and equipment links

This is recommended as either a standalone application to link with any camera, or as an integrated imaging tool with Acuitas
Clinical, and is for those opticians who:

Want - a robust and feature rich digital imaging software solution from a software company, need reassurance that the images
over time will be available in one search, want guaranteed continuity of upgrades, realise the value of support from the original
manufacturer, see the value in integrated imaging with paperless medical record, require one database for consolidation of all
imaging and equipment data.

Do not want – to have to review images over many CDs and fear restrictions to cameras or equipment suppliers.

Acuitas DR Screening – local or national image capture, grading and administration
Acuitas DR Screening:
  • Image capture, storage and transfer and initial grading
  • Grading, local or central
  • Administration and Managed services
