Innovations performs all surface and finish layout calculations automatically. A comprehensive database includes manufacturer
-supplied curves, surfacing charts, and topographical maps for accurate processing of single vision, multifocal, and aspheric
lenses. Lens selection can take place automatically based on prescription, frame trace, and material preference. Detailed
machine settings can be printed or sent electronically to machine interfaces.

Lens Calculation and Processing features include:

Master lens database
This database allows automatic blank selection and inventory control by selecting appropriate lenses and displaying stock
status. The data is refreshed in periodic lens updates that are included with annual service support contracts.

Adaptable lens selection
Innovations provides flexible lens selection by using a three-pass selection process as it searches for the most appropriate
stocked lens for the prescription and frame. When no lenses in a particular category are in-stock, lab-specified "preferred
alternates" are chosen instead. If an appropriate blank cannot be found, Innovations displays the largest available blank
and the frame trace, allowing the operator to move the lens in the frame to achieve cut-out, then automatically recalculates
PD and seg height.

Centralized data management
When new lenses are introduced, automatic updates to your database will be available on CD-ROM or via e-mail. Soon,
updates will be available automatically over the Internet.

Base curve selection
Base curve selection charts can be modified to suit lab preferences or to select a flatter base curve as is often needed
for half-eye glasses.

Lens Explorer format
The lens database is presented in the same way files are shown in WindowsÂ® Explorer. Access to lens data is more intuitive
and settings for each category are easier to manage.

Online help
Online help provides explanations of terms and procedures.

Comprehensive worktickets
Innovations worktickets show settings for most equipment. The level of detail and layout can be modified to serve the needs
of individual labs.

Customized worktickets
The workticket designer enables customization of existing worktickets or the production of entirely new designs. The workticket
browser allows you to browse on-screen all of the worktickets produced for each order.
