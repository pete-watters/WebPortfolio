Acuitas Enterprise is more than a software package – it’s a concept, an architecture, a way to structure and distribute
information.

It’s an instrument to give you Global Visibility over your chain of stores, and to empower your stores to make the most of
the affiliation to your Group.
  • It’s a business model
    Acuitas Enterprise facilitates uniformity across all store locations because it controls (or, more accurately, can control) that
    all stores see (or can see) the same examination list, use the same recall and referral letters, share the contents of various
    drop down lists and categories (such as hobbies, medical and marketing categories, examination results, etc.). Additionally,
    it provides facilities whereby stock, promotions, discounts, etc, can be configured and controlled centrally. Your Enterprise
    implementation is a project for Ocuco. Your specific sharing and control requirements are agreed in advance during the
    planning phase.
  • It’s a technical solution
    Technically, the enterprise environment is achieved by use of what is known as ‘replicatio’. This is an Oracle database
    facility whereby each store can have its own separate database (to which their store application runs and stores information),
    and that that database is a sub-set of a ‘master’ database existing at the head office. The head office contains all data from
    all stores – and it is then the process of replicating data which sends information up from the stores to head office and down
    to the stores from the head office.
  • It’s a set of software functionality features
    There are implications of running in an enterprise environment within the Acuitas application itself. In some instances, this
    is the provision of additional features and functionality at either or both the head office and the local stores. In some instances,
    this is the prevention of certain functions in the local stores which would be available in a stand-alone system.
  • It’s a state of mind
    By its nature, Enterprise forces a paradigm shift in thinking, away from the stores trading individually into a thought process
    of operating the business in a multi-location, distributed database environment. You will take on board certain characteristics
    of the business and the positive functional impact that the Enterprise environment causes.
Some of the questions on how your organisation will get the most out of Acuitas Enterprise can typically only be answered
by you – but Ocuco can help you finding those answers by our extensive experience of Enterprise requirements analysis and
roll-outs large and small.
