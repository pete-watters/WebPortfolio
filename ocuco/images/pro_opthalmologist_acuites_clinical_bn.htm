Overview
Acuitas changes the usual practice with NHS forms as it does not require the preparation of forms in advance. It saves staff time
and effort by only handling the information once. The Rx is entered by the OO in the Rx page and it is then merged into the NHS
form along with any other relevant information. Acuitas users generally print the NHS form on a multi-tray duplex printer as part
of the handover. This allows them to sign the forms straight away and handover with the patient to the DO.
Features
  • Completes all information required on the NHS forms
  • No need for hand writing additional information
  • Prompts to gather correct information
Benefits
  • Saves administration staff time as preparation not required
  • Signature of OO does not have to wait until end of day
  • Reduces returns from claiming authority due to illegibility
Tracking Benefits
Key Features
  • Easy to manage benefits
  • Reduces patient balance automatically
  • Any number of institutions can be tracked
  • Auto-update facility for payments
Key Benefits
  • Clear information to institutions leads to quicker payments
  • Completes the patient journey
